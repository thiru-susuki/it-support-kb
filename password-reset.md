# Password Reset Issues

## Problem
User is unable to login because they forgot their password or account is locked.

## Solution

### Step 1 — Self Service Reset
- Go to your company login page
- Click "Forgot Password"
- Enter your registered email address
- Check your inbox for reset link
- Click the link and set a new password

### Step 2 — IT Support Reset
If self service does not work:
- Raise a ticket with IT Support
- Provide your Employee ID and Department
- IT team will reset within 2 business hours

### Step 3 — Account Locked
If account is locked after multiple wrong attempts:
- Wait 30 minutes for auto unlock
- Or contact IT Support immediately

## Prevention Tips
- Change password every 90 days
- Use strong passwords (8+ characters, numbers, symbols)
- Never share your password with anyone
