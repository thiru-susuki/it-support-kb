# Laptop Not Starting

## Problem
User's laptop is not turning on, stuck on black screen, 
or not booting properly.

## Solution

### Step 1 — Basic Power Check
- Make sure laptop is charged or plugged in
- Check power adapter and cable for damage
- Try a different power outlet
- Hold power button for 10 seconds to force restart

### Step 2 — Remove External Devices
- Disconnect all USB devices, external monitors
- Remove any connected headphones or dongles
- Try powering on again

### Step 3 — Battery Reset
- Shutdown the laptop completely
- Remove battery if removable
- Hold power button for 30 seconds
- Reinsert battery and try again

### Step 4 — Safe Mode Boot
- Press power button
- Immediately press F8 or F11 repeatedly
- Select "Safe Mode" from boot menu
- If boots in safe mode — software issue exists

### Step 5 — Contact IT Support
If laptop still does not start:
- Do NOT try to repair yourself
- Raise urgent ticket with IT Support
- IT team will collect and repair within 1 business day

## Warning Signs to Report Immediately
- Burning smell from laptop
- Cracked screen or physical damage
- Laptop fell or got wet
